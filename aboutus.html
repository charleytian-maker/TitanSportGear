Company Introduction

TitanSportGear is a sporting goods brand specializing in ball sports equipment, pickleball paddles and sets, fishing lures and tackle, and pet products — built to serve players, retailers, and distributors across North America, Europe, and Australia. We are a foreign trade company with nearly ten years of experience exporting sports equipment from China to international markets.

What started as a traditional ball sports export business has grown into a focused, design-driven brand. As demand shifted, we shifted with it — investing in newer, fast-growing categories like pickleball, fishing gear, and pet products, while keeping the manufacturing relationships, quality control, and export expertise we built over the years.

Our Story

Sport brings people together, and the right equipment makes the game better. That belief drives everything we do at TitanSportGear. After years of supplying sports equipment to international buyers, we saw an opportunity: athletes and weekend players deserved gear that combined factory-direct pricing with genuine attention to performance and design — not just generic, mass-produced products.

So we built TitanSportGear to do exactly that: source, design, and deliver equipment that performs as well as it looks, backed by a team that understands both manufacturing and the markets we sell into.

What We Offer

  • Pickleball Paddles & Sets — From entry-level sets to performance paddles for competitive play, our pickleball line is built for the sport's fast-growing community of players in the US, Canada, and Europe.
  • Fishing Lures & Tackle — A growing range of fishing lures and tackle designed for durability and performance on the water, for both casual anglers and serious enthusiasts.
  • Ball Sports Equipment — Basketballs, volleyballs, and beach sports equipment backed by years of manufacturing and export experience in traditional ball sports categories.
  • Pet Products — We're expanding into pet products, bringing the same attention to quality and design to gear for the animals who are part of our customers' active lifestyles.

We supply both B2B buyers — retailers, distributors, and wholesalers looking for reliable supply chains and OEM/ODM options — and B2C customers shopping directly through our online storefronts.

Materials We Use

We select premium raw materials tailored for different sporting goods categories to balance durability, performance and environmental friendliness. - Pickleball Paddles: We adopt high-strength carbon fiber, lightweight honeycomb polymer core and anti-abrasion surface coating, delivering strong impact resistance and stable hitting performance. - Sport Balls: Multi-layer composite leather, high-elastic inner rubber bladder and air-locking lining are applied, ensuring consistent bounce, soft touch and excellent air tightness under various scenarios. - Flying Discs & Outdoor Gear: Flexible impact-resistant PP composite materials are used, featuring good toughness, UV resistance and long service life for outdoor activities. All materials pass preliminary inspection before production. We prioritize wear resistance, safe composition and eco-friendly properties to meet global wholesale and customization demands.

Quality Control & Certifications

Strict quality control runs through the whole manufacturing process. We conduct incoming raw material inspection, random in-line sampling during production, and full inspection for finished goods before packaging. Defective items will be separated and reworked to keep consistent product quality for every shipment.

Our products comply with international safety standards. We have obtained relevant test reports such as SGS. We are able to arrange third-party testing according to customers’ market requirements to satisfy different regional import regulations.

Customer Support

We provide multilingual customer service in English, German and Chinese. Our team strives to reply to all inquiries within 24 working hours. If you receive defective goods caused by production issues, please contact us with photos for after-sales solutions. We will actively communicate to handle quality complaints reasonably and safeguard your business interests.
